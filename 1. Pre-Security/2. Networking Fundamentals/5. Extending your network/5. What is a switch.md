A switch is a dedicated networking device responsible for providing a means of connecting to multiple devices. Switches can facilitate many devices (from 3 to 63) using Ethernet cables.

There are layer2 and layer3 switches

## layer 2 switches
These switches are solely responsible for sending frames to the correct device.
Only forwards further the frames(the ip is encapsulated within the frames)

## layer 3 switches
These switches are more sophisticated than layer 2, as they can perform _some_ of the responsibilities of a router.
These switches send frames to devices (as layer 2 does) and route packets to other devices using the IP protoco
